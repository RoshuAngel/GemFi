About GemFI

GemFI is GameFi involving SVG NFTs with a chance of winning the POT money
How to play?

  • Mint 3 Gem starting with level 1

  • Upgrade 3 for 1 Gem with a chance of upgrade failing

  • Failed upgrade will mint 1 Gem with same level and balance

  • Balance from 2nd Gem paid to User

  • Balance from 3rd Gem goes to Gem Pot

  • Successful upgrade will mint 1 Gem with 3x Balance and +1 Level

  • Upgrade Gems to get higher level for entry on Gem Pot Board

  • Winner of Gem pot picked randomly from Gem Pot Board

  • Any eligible user from leader board can call the pickWinner function every 24hr.
